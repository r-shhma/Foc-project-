Health is one of the most important aspects of a person’s life, as it affects both physical and mental well-being. 
Maintaining good health involves eating a balanced diet, exercising regularly, getting enough sleep, and managing stress effectively. 
When a person is healthy, they can perform daily activities efficiently and enjoy a better quality of life. Good health also 
helps prevent diseases and strengthens the body’s ability to fight infections. Therefore, taking care of one’s health should 
always be a top priority for living a happy and productive life.
